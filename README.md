Politicards turns dry government data into something anyone can actually understand.

Every member of Congress gets their own trading card — complete with real voting records, bill history, attendance scores, and bipartisan ratings pulled directly from official U.S. government sources. No middlemen. No editorial spin. Just the facts.
What's on every card:

Search any member of Congress by name or state and instantly see their attendance record, how often they cross party lines, how aligned they are with their party, how many bills they've sponsored and co-sponsored, and their signature legislation.

Where the data comes from:
Everything in Politicards is sourced from the Library of Congress Congress.gov API, the U.S. Senate, and the Office of the Clerk of the U.S. House of Representatives. It's updated daily. It's public domain. It's official.

Our commitment:
We don't tell you what to think. A 95% party-line score isn't labeled good or bad — it's just a number. Attendance, bipartisan crossover, bills passed — these are behavioral facts. You decide what they mean.

Take action:
Every card links directly to Legisletter so you can contact your representative about the votes that matter to you.

Politicards is free, contains no ads, and collects zero personal data.
