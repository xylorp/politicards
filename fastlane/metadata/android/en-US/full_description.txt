Politicards turns every member of the U.S. Congress into a trading card.

Browse all 538 senators and representatives — each with their photo, party, state, committee roles, and live voting record stats displayed in a clean card format.

Features:
• All 535 voting members of the House and Senate plus non-voting delegates
• Real-time vote sync — cards reflect the latest roll call data
• Stat meters showing voting participation and party-line trends
• Filter and search by state, party, or chamber
• Clean, minimal design with dark mode support

Politicards is an independent, open-source civics tool with no ads, no tracking, and no accounts required. Data is sourced from public government records.

Source code is available on GitHub under the GPL-3.0 license.
