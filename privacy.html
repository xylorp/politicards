POLITICARDS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Politicards does not collect, store, or share any personal data about its users. Ever.

Overview

Politicards ("we", "our", or "the app") is a civic data tool that displays publicly available information about members of the United States Congress. We are committed to your privacy. This policy describes what data we do and do not collect.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

— Your name, email address, or any contact information

— Your location or IP address

— Device identifiers or advertising IDs

— Usage analytics or behavior tracking

— Search queries you enter in the app

— Any personally identifiable information of any kind

Data sources

All congressional data displayed in Politicards is sourced from official U.S. government sources, including the Congress.gov API (Library of Congress), the U.S. Senate, and the Office of the Clerk of the U.S. House of Representatives. This data is public domain.

Third-party services

Politicards links to Legisletter.org to allow users to contact their representatives. When you follow that link, you leave Politicards and are subject to Legisletter's own privacy policy.

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may collect standard web request data (such as IP address) as part of serving those fonts. See Google's privacy policy.

Cookies and local storage

Politicards does not use cookies. The app does not use local storage or any browser-based storage mechanism to store user data.

Children's privacy

Politicards does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data whatsoever, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we collect no data, any future changes would only occur if we add features that change that.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? You can reach us at privacy@politicards.com.
